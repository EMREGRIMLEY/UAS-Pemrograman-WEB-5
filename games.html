Project Motor Racing

Project Motor Racing is currently in development for PC and consoles and is scheduled for release in 2025. Based on the GIANTS Engine, the highly realistic racing simulation game is being developed by Straight4 the makers of some of the most acclaimed titles in the genre. PMR will replicate the challenge and sensory overload of professional-level race cars with all the uncompromising passion of big-time motorsport.

GIANTS Software and Straight4 Studios have been working together since 2023 to integrate the GIANTS Engine into Project Motor Racing, after establishing a technical partnership to create high quality simulation games.
